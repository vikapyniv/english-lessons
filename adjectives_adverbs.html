Discover the pattern

Meet two different colleagues, notice the grammar, and build the rule yourself.

1Two very different colleagues

Read the story, then discuss the questions on the right. Do not analyse the grammar yet.

Anna is a careful person. She always plans her work carefully. She speaks quietly and listens patiently.

Tom is energetic and fast. He works fast and makes decisions quickly. However, he sometimes does things badly because he does not check his work carefully.

Discuss

Who would you prefer to work with?Explain your choice.
