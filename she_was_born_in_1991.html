Famous lives

Famous people by profession

Life stories

5

Your life in conversation

Choose several categories. Tell your partner what happened, when it happened and why it mattered. Use these linkers: first · then · after that · later · finally

Keep the conversation going:
When did it happen? · Where were you? · Who was with you? · Why did it matter? · What happened next? · How did you feel?
6

Life-changing events discussion

Discuss one question at a time. Give a developed answer and ask your partner two follow-up questions.

Which decision changed your life the most?
Use Past Simple to tell a complete story, not just a single sentence.
