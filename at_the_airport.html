Airport riddles

Read, type and check.

I carry a bag and sometimes feel stressed. I look at big screens to find where to go next. I show a document with my name and flight, then I sit and wait before I fly.

You pack me before you go away, with clothes and things for every day. I travel on my wheels near and far, then wait for you where the others are.

You show me before you get on the plane. I have your photo, your number and name. Without me, they won't let you fly.
